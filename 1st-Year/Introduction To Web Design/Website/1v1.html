Fishing Rod

What is the Fishing Rod used for?

The fishing rod is used for knockback and combos so it allows the person using the fishing rod to hit the opponent but makes it harder for the opponent to hit the person using the fishing rod. It is also beneficial when running to regenerate health.

Bow

Using the bow is great for the start of the fight to get some early damage, then later rush in and start the main part of the fight to obtain a slight damage advantage.

Sword

Block hitting

Block hitting reduces the taken damage. To do this you need to click both left click and right click whilst fighting, the right click blocks and left click attacks so by clicking both whilst fighting (prioritising left click) you can minimise the amount of damage taken from a fight. To reduce damage even more try strafing too.

Practice Makes Perfect

Training at 1v1 forms of combat will help you in all other forms of combat mentioned on this website.
