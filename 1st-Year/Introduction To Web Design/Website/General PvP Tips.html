Strafing

What Is Strafing?

Strafing is dodging from left to right making it harder to be hit.


How to Strafe

To strafe, you would use the "A" and "D" key while fighting, doing this will make you harder to hit whilst hitting the opponent.

Clicking Techniques

Clicking Techniques are very important to increase a players CPS (Clicks Per Second). This is important so the player can be able to deal more damage faster and also dodge some hits.

Jitter Click

Jitter clicking is clicking while slightly moving your mouse, this appears to the player that you are moving your head, using this clicking technique also alters your hitbox and dodges some hits.

Butterfly Clicking

This method is banned on certain PvP servers so be careful when using this technique.

Butterfly clicking increases the player's CPS (Click Per Second) drastically and this results in doing more damage than other players that use standard clicking methods. To do this method you place your first two fingers on the left click button of the mouse and your third finger on the right click of the mouse.
